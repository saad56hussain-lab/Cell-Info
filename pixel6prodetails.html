Google Pixel 6 Pro

Google Pixel 6 Pro marks a major shift for Google smartphones with the introduction of the custom Tensor processor. This phone is designed to enhance AI-driven features, photography, and system performance. The large LTPO OLED display provides vibrant colors and smooth scrolling, making it ideal for productivity and entertainment. The triple-camera system significantly improves photography, offering better zoom, detail, and low-light performance. Pixel 6 Pro also focuses on security and long-term software support, ensuring the device remains reliable over time. Its premium design and powerful hardware make it suitable for demanding users.

  • Storage : Available in 128GB, 256GB, and 512GB
  • Processor : Google Tensor
  • Display : 6.7-inch LTPO OLED display
  • Camera : 50MP (Wide) + 48MP (Telephoto) + 12MP (Ultra Wide)
  • Battery : 5003 mAh
  • Operating System : Android
  • User Experience : Pixel 6 Pro delivers intelligent performance, powerful photography, and a premium Android experience with long-term support.
