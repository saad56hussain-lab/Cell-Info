Google Pixel 7 Pro

Google Pixel 7 Pro refines the Pixel experience with improved efficiency and performance through the Tensor G2 processor. The device offers smoother multitasking and enhanced AI features compared to its predecessor. The large OLED display remains sharp and fluid, providing an immersive viewing experience. Camera performance is further refined with improved image processing and consistent results across different lenses. Pixel 7 Pro focuses on reliability, stability, and smart features that enhance everyday usage. It is well-suited for users who want a balanced flagship Android smartphone.

  • Storage : Available in 128GB, 256GB, and 512GB
  • Processor : Google Tensor G2
  • Display : 6.7-inch LTPO OLED display
  • Camera : 50MP (Wide) + 48MP (Telephoto) + 12MP (Ultra Wide)
  • Battery : 5000 mAh
  • Operating System : Android
  • User Experience : Pixel 7 Pro offers a refined, responsive, and dependable experience with strong performance and camera consistency.
