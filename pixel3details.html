Google Pixel 3

$799

Google Pixel 3 builds upon the strengths of its predecessor by offering improved display quality, better speakers, and enhanced camera software. The phone focuses on delivering a premium Android experience with Google’s smart AI features integrated deeply into the system. The OLED display produces vibrant colors and sharp details, making it suitable for watching videos and browsing content. Pixel 3 is especially known for its photography capabilities, delivering impressive results in both daylight and low-light conditions. Google’s image processing allows the camera to capture detailed photos without relying on multiple lenses. With timely updates and a refined design, Pixel 3 remains a solid option for users who value simplicity and camera quality.

  • Storage : Available in 64GB and 128GB
  • Processor : Snapdragon 845
  • Display : 5.5-inch OLED display
  • Camera : 12.2MP (Wide)
  • Battery : 2915 mAh
  • Operating System : Android
  • User Experience : Pixel 3 offers a clean and fluid Android experience with strong camera performance and consistent day-to-day reliability.
